What is F3?

F3 is a free-to-lead workout for men, by men. But F3 is not just a workout group. It’s a bond of faith and fellowship.
Thus, the three F’s stand for fitness, faith, and fellowship.
Below is a quick getting started video we would like you to watch.

F3 has five core principles:

1. ​Free of charge. There is never a price to work out, ever.
2. ​Open to all men. No matter your background, you are welcome at F3.
3. ​Held outdoors. Rain or shine, heat or chill, we’ll see you in the gloom.
4. ​Peer led. The men of F3 lead one another on a rotating basis.
5. ​Ends with a circle of trust. Always.
